Project Objective:

As an avid open-source contributor, I created a clock design and named it Timeless. This project represents my contribution in the Animation Nation, a Zero to Mastery Academy challenge for Hacktoberfest. This and other open-source projects from Zero to Mastery Academy bring students together in a collaborative coding symphony. Timeless speaks volumes about my GitHub skills, creativity, adherence to open-source coding guidelines and proficiency in creating smooth, simple animations using HTML and CSS. The Animation-Nation platform serves not only as a playground for fun-coding for Zero to Mastery Academy students but also demonstrates the power of shared knowledge and the collective strength that emerges when individuals integrate their diverse mindsets.
