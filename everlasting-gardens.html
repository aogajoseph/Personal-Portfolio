Objective:

The Everlasting Gardens website project aimed to elevate the elegant guest house's online presence. With a focus on visually captivating and user-friendly interfaces, a responsive design, intuitive navigation and integrated booking functionality, I crafted seamless browsing experiences to help guests explore accommodations, check availability and make reservations effortlessly from any device. Through immersive visual storytelling, interactive virtual tours and curated guides to the local surroundings, I aimed to inspire and inform prospective guests while showcasing the accommodation's unique offerings and the surrounding destination's allure. I utilized multilingual support to ensure inclusion for international visitors, enhancing the accommodation's global appeal. The project's successful execution has garnered positive feedback, positioning the Everlasting Gardens as a premier destination for guests in Kenya.
